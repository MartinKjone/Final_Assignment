The problem

We wish to contribute to solving of the second UN SDG Goal, zero hunger and will be contributing by trying to reduce wastage.

Food is a finite resource many Norwegians take for granted. Norwegians are amongst the most wasteful of people and hence contribute to the food shortage starving many around the globe. Calculations done by matvett.no tells us that Norwegians waste about 385 000 metric ton on a yearly basis and that the general population is to be blamed for 58% of this waste. In conclusion, on average a Norwegian citizen wastes 42,5 kg of edible food every year. This 58% wastage could feed half a million people and reduce world hunger.
